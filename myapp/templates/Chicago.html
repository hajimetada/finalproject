Chicago Neighborhoods and community areas

Chicago is made up of 77 community areas that are often grouped into 9 districts or “sides.” The community areas are well defined and do not overlap. Each community area has one or more neighborhoods in it. The neighborhoods are often not well defined and over lap each other. The section "Community Areas" describes the relationship between neighborhoods and community areas.
