Chicago Neighborhoods and Community Areas

Chicago is made up of 77 community areas that are often grouped into 9 districts or “sides”.The community areas are well defined and do not overlap with each other. Each community area has one or more neighborhoods in it. On the other hand, the neighborhoods are often not well defined and sometimes overlap with each other.
